General Information

  • The first stage will be an online elimination round. This stage will be live for 5 days (5th May 2019 to 9th May 2019) and will have seven questions.
  • The second stage will be an on-site round. This stage will be live for 3 hours and will have three questions.
  • Selection of the candidates for the second stage will be notified later.
  • Number of candidates selected for the second stage and the time and venue for the same will be notified once the first stage ends.

Platform Overview

  • In order to submit a solution, please write your code in your system IDE/Editor, then upload your file.
  • Allowed languages are C, C++, Python 2 and Python 3 .
  • Your rank is being calculated based on your score and total time you have taken to achieve the score.

Code of Conduct

  • The candidate's submission code should not match with any other candidate's code. Plagarism is strictly prohibited!
  • The candidate himself is participating in the contest and not someone else on his behalf.

Disclaimer

  • In case of any conflict, the final decision will be taken by the Code Warrior Team. No further questions or challenges will be entertained.
  • The Code Warrior Team reserves the right to disqualify any candidate who fails to comply with the Code of Conduct.
